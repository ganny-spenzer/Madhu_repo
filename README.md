# Romantic Gift Website - Cinderella Theme

## How to Add Your Cinderella Images

You need 3 beautiful Cinderella images in pink tones:

### Image 1: Cinderella in Difficult Times (page2.html)
- Shows Cinderella in rags or working
- Save as: `cinderella1.jpg` or `cinderella1.png`

### Image 2: Cinderella Transforming (page3.html)
- Shows Cinderella's magical transformation
- Save as: `cinderella2.jpg` or `cinderella2.png`

### Image 3: Cinderella as Princess (page4.html)
- Shows Cinderella in her ball gown at the palace
- Save as: `cinderella3.jpg` or `cinderella3.png`

## Where to Get Cinderella Images

Use these prompts in AI art generators like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Leonardo.ai:

**For Image 1:**
```
Cinderella in torn pink dress holding a broom, sad but hopeful expression, cottage background, soft pink color palette, fairy tale illustration style, beautiful detailed art
```

**For Image 2:**

```
Cinderella surrounded by magical sparkles and pink light, dress transforming, fairy godmother magic, pink and purple colors, enchanting atmosphere, fairy tale art
```

## Installation Steps

1. Get your 3 Cinderella images
2. Rename them to:
   - `cinderella1.jpg` (or .png)
   - `cinderella2.jpg` (or .png)
   - `cinderella3.jpg` (or .png)
3. Place them in the same folder as your HTML files
4. Refresh your browser
